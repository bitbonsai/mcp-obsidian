# MCP-Obsidian

> Universal AI Bridge for Obsidian Vaults

MCP-Obsidian is an open-source Model Context Protocol (MCP) server that gives AI assistants safe, intelligent access to Obsidian vaults. Works with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini CLI, and any MCP-compatible platform. No plugins required, no Obsidian running needed.
